The New Hospital Programme (NHP) Demand Model

  • An award-winning, fully open source model for estimating future hospital activity1
  • Used for local hospital planning and to inform national policy

The model in a nutshell

Probabilistic Monte Carlo simulation model, providing hundreds of possible futures and an average forecast
